Our Story

In 2010, in the lively lanes of Pune's Rasta Peth, Siddhu opened Siddhu's Maharashtrian Thali with one clear mission: to serve the most authentic Maharashtrian thali in the city. Drawing from his mother and grandmother's treasured recipes, he brought to every steel plate the true taste of home — smoky pithla with crisp bhakri, fiery thecha, comforting varan-bhaat, and the indulgent sweetness of puran poli with sajuk tup.

The thali quickly became our signature, loved for its generous portions, perfect balance of flavours, and the feeling of a complete, heartfelt Maharashtrian meal. Over the years, we've grown with Pune's families and students, staying true to fresh, daily-ground masalas and traditional cooking.

Besides our beloved thalis, we also run a popular monthly mess service, delivering wholesome, home-style food to hostels and working professionals who crave ghar ka khana without the hassle. Fifteen years on, whether you join us for a quick thali or sign up for our mess, you're always family. Anna hava? Come, taste Maharashtra's warmth on every plate.
